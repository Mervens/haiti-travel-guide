Travel to Port-Au-Prince

Capital of the Carribean island of Haiti, Port-Au-Prince is a busy and intricate city at the home and heart of Haitian civilization.

Best Parts of Haitian Culture from a Glance

Eat at the fantastic Haitian restaraunts. A mix of Spanish, French, and custom meals that satisfy your taste buds in every bite.

Go site see at famous Haitian art and architecture at our national museums. Explore the history of the nation that started the slave revolution.

Experience luxurious life without extreme wealth. The Haitian currency is worth 1/5th other populations, increasing your networth many times over.
